Our History

"Makeys" was born out of the project "¡Supérate! Emprende" . After considering several ideas, such as spicy apples and erasable pens, they opted for personalized key chains after a survey on social networks. The company specializes in creating emotional keychains that capture special stories and memories, being meaningful gifts for important moments. "Makeys" not only sells products, but shares stories, reflecting its essence in the "love of memories".
